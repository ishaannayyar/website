About

I left India a decade ago — and I'm still navigating borders.

Since I landed in Europe, I've been juggling savings in multiple currencies, watching a tech career compound across continents, and answering the same question every few years: where exactly is home, and where should it be next?

Most professional advice is built for people who stay still. Single country. Single currency. Single career arc. International life doesn't work that way.

So I built my own frameworks for the three areas that matter most: how to save and invest when your money lives across borders, how to grow a career that travels with you, and how to navigate the visa, residency and identity questions that come with all of it.

This is education, not advice. I won't tell you what to do. I'll teach you how to think — so you can ask better questions of the licensed advisors who eventually do.
